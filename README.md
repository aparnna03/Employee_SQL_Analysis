# Employee_SQL_Analysis
SQL analysis project on employee dataset using MySQL

### Dataset Columns  
**Education**, **JoiningYear**, **City**, **PaymentTier**, **Age**, **Gender**, **ExperienceInCurrentDomain**

### Key Queries  
- Average experience by city  
- Average payment tier by education  
- Employee count by payment tier  
- Average age by gender  
- Cities with the highest average payment tier  
- Education levels where average age > 25  
- Gender distribution in each city  

### General Insights  
- Most employees have around **3 years of experience** across cities. 
- Bangalore is the city with highest average payment Tier.
- Maximum employees joined in **2017**, indicating a **major hiring phase** that year.   
- The **average age** of employees generally falls between **25â€“30 years**.  
- **Bachelors** and **Masters** degree holders dominate the workforce.  
- The gender distribution is relatively **balanced** across most cities.  
- The **youngest** employee across all education levels is **22** years old.
